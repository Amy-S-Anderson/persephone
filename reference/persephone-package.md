# persephone: Agent-Based Model for Simulating Skeletal Lesion Formation and Mortality in Archaeological Populations

An agent-based model (ABM) for simulating skeletal assemblage data in
bioarchaeology. Individuals in a birth cohort face annual risks of
skeletal lesion formation and age-specific mortality following a Siler
hazard model. Lesion status can modify mortality risk, enabling
exploration of the osteological paradox and evaluation of survival
analysis methods applied to cemetery data.
